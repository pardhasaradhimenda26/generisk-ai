# GeneRisk AI Redesign Walkthrough

The GeneRisk AI platform has been redesigned with a modern dark techy aesthetic, focusing on clinical precision and professional clarity.

## 🧬 DNA Helix Enhancement
The background DNA helix has been refined to be more subtle and professional.
- **Opacity**: Reduced to `0.12` on home and `0.06` on other sections.
- **Colors**: Deep techy teals (`0x003344`, `0x004455`).
- **Brightness**: 60% reduction for better readability.

## 🎨 Color Scheme & Typography
- **Primary Background**: `#080C14`
- **Accent Colors**: `#00D4FF` (Teal) and `#00FF88` (Green)
- **Typography**: 'Space Grotesk' (Headings) and 'Inter' (Body).

## 🏗️ Layout Components
- **Navbar**: Sleek glassmorphism header.
- **Hero Section**: Grid-pattern background with glowing orb effect.
- **Cards & Badges**: Updated with glassmorphism and tech-themed risk levels.

## 📊 New Features
- **Algorithm Table**: Comparison of ML models with clinical precision.
- **Structured Footer**: Professional footer with navigation and legal sections.

## 🧪 Verification
Redesign verified on both desktop and mobile layouts for consistency and performance. Changes have been pushed to GitHub.
